Steve Jobs

The man who revolutionized the personal computer

Steven Paul Jobs was an American business magnate, inventor, and investor.

Here's the timeline of his life

  • 1955 - Born on February 24 in San Francisco, California.
  • 1972 - Graduated from Homestead High School in Cupertino, California and enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon. He dropped out after one semester.
  • 1974 - Traveled to India in search of spiritual enlightenment and experimented with psychedelics.
  • 1976 - Co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne on April 1. They released the Apple I computer.
  • 1985 - Jobs resigned from Apple after losing a power struggle with the board of directors.
  • 1986 - Acquired The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar Animation Studios) from Lucasfilm for $10 million.
  • 1997 - Returned to Apple after Apple acquired NeXT. Jobs became Apple's interim CEO.
  • 2007 - Apple introduced the iPhone, revolutionizing the smartphone industry.
  • 2011 - Passed away on October 5 due to complications from a rare form of pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
